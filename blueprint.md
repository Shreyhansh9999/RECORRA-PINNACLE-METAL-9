# **App Name**: Pinnacle Metals

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Management & Display: Flexible system for updating and displaying all static content including company overview, vision, values, global reach details, and 'Why Choose Us' section.
- Interactive Hero Slider: A full-width, auto-sliding image carousel on the homepage showcasing key company messages and industrial imagery, featuring clear text overlays and call-to-action buttons (e.g., 'Request a Quote', 'Contact Us').
- Metal & Service Catalog Display: Clearly presents available metal types (Steel, Aluminum, Copper, Scrap, etc.) and industrial services (Import, Export, Logistics, Sourcing) in an organized, image-rich grid format with brief descriptions.
- Quote Request & Contact Forms: Dedicated forms for users to submit quote requests with fields for material, quantity, location, and contact information, alongside a general contact form.
- Direct Communication Integrations: Integrates prominent contact details and direct links for instant communication via phone, email, and a floating WhatsApp chat button for quick inquiries and support.
- AI-Powered Metal Selector Tool: An AI-driven tool where users can describe their industrial requirements, and the AI will suggest optimal metal types from SREE RECORRA's catalog, detailing their properties and ideal applications.
- Global Presence Visualizer: A visual representation (e.g., interactive world map or shipping routes graphic) demonstrating the company's extensive global reach and logistical capabilities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#C9A34E). This premium gold defines the brand's luxury and trust, used for prominent headings, interactive elements, and key brand accents.
- Background colors: Deep Black/Dark Grey (#1A1A1A) forms the sophisticated primary background for dramatic contrast, complemented by Pure White (#FFFFFF) for clean, highly readable content sections.
- Accent color: Dark Gold (#A67C2D). This rich, darker shade of gold provides elegant contrast and is reserved for hover states, subtle dividers, icons, and intricate design details, enhancing the premium feel without being flashy.
- Headline font: 'Playfair Display' (serif), an elegant choice that reflects a luxury and high-end aesthetic. Body font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif), providing a modern, clean, and highly readable foundation for all textual content. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Icons will adopt a minimal, refined style, predominantly rendered in the gold theme to maintain brand consistency and elevate the premium aesthetic.
- A sophisticated and clean layout featuring generous section spacing, full-width high-quality industrial images, thin gold dividers, and a sticky navigation bar for ease of access and a premium user experience.
- Subtle and tasteful animations are preferred, including smooth scrolling and elegant hover effects using the dark gold accent color for interactive elements, avoiding anything flashy or distracting.
